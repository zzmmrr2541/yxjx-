PPOJECT HIGHLIGHTS

Innovation pioneered Bancor 2.0

The concept of Bancor2.0 was first proposed and practiced by ET, aiming to solve the problem of insufficient depth under the bancor protocol and the inability to delegate pending orders. With the open reserve management pool, anyone can put their idle funds into the management pool and enjoy the fee income; the online commissioning order mechanism allows the user to perform the pending order operation.

Open source open chain

ET is the first trading platform in the world to promote open autonomy. All front-end and back-end code is open source. All interfaces are open. All existing trading platforms can be seamlessly connected and enjoy trading on their platforms. The profit generated.

EosToken wallet as a traffic portal

As the first EOS-based wallet, EosToken has attracted many professional eos users since its launch. At present, there are more than 500,000 wallet users and the highest daily life is 40,000.

Wallet entrance is safe and reliable

ET is a trading platform built into the wallet APP. It can be used directly on the wallet APP. There is no risk of revealing the private key, which is very convenient and safe.

Smart contracts are transparent
